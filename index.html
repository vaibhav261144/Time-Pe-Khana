About Us

At Time Pe Khana, we believe that great dining experiences should be as easy to access as they are to enjoy. Whether you're looking for a cozy spot for two or planning a special celebration, our platform connects you to real-time table availability at your favorite restaurants. Our mission is to take the guesswork out of dining by providing a seamless booking experience that fits your schedule. With just a few clicks, you can discover new culinary gems or secure a table at your go-to spots—all in one convenient place. We’re passionate about supporting both diners and restaurants, making it easier than ever to enjoy great food without the hassle. Whether you're planning ahead or looking for a last-minute reservation, we've got you covered. Welcome to effortless dining—your table is just a click away!
